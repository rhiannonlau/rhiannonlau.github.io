Project: Queen's Community Engagment Record

Type: Capstone project

Link: communityengagementrecord.azurewebsites.net (must have a Queen's account to access)

Technical details:

  • Group members: Three other Queen's students
  • Languages: Python, MySQL, HTML, CSS
  • Framework: Flask
  • Techniques:

Description:

The Community Engagment Record is a web application for Queen's students that allows them to log their extra-curricular activities and set goals and reflections about these activities to create a portfolio that can be downloaded as a PDF and sent to employers or used in applications. In addition, students can complete workplace skill self-assessments and keep track of their progress in skill areas over time. Finally, administrators can log in to their accounts and view web analytics/statistics of student activity and engagement with the CER, as well as view all the users in the databases and give/revoke admin privileges to other users.
